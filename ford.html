Bronco Sport

he Ford Bronco Sport is a compact SUV built for adventure, combining rugged off-road capability with everyday convenience. It features standard 4x4, multiple G.O.A.T. (Goes Over Any Terrain) modes, and a powerful EcoBoost engine. Inside, it offers a spacious cabin with versatile storage solutions, perfect for outdoor enthusiasts. Equipped with Ford Co-Pilot360™ safety features, the Bronco Sport ensures confidence on any journey, whether it's tackling rough trails or city streets. Stylish, practical, and ready for adventure, the Bronco Sport is a true companion for explorers.

The Bronco Sport is equipped with a turbocharged engine lineup, offering both the 1.5L EcoBoost 3-cylinder and the more powerful 2.0L EcoBoost 4-cylinder. Paired with a smooth 8-speed automatic transmission and standard 4x4 capability, this SUV handles tough trails and inclement weather with ease. For added durability and traction, higher trims include advanced features like G.O.A.T. (Goes Over Any Terrain) modes, Trail Control, and all-terrain tires.

Mustang

The 2025 Ford Mustang offers three engine options: a 2.3L EcoBoost Turbocharged I-4 with 310 hp, a 5.0L Coyote V8 with 450 hp (GT), and a 5.0L Coyote V8 with 500 hp (Dark Horse). It features a 10-speed automatic transmission and rear-wheel drive. Dimensions include a length of 188.5 inches, a width of 75.4 inches, and a height of 55.1 inches, with a wheelbase of 107.1 inches and a cargo capacity of 13.4 cubic feet. The fuel tank capacity is 16 gallons.

The Mustang seats four and comes equipped with a 13.2-inch color multi-function touch screen display, SYNC® 4, wireless Apple CarPlay™, Android Auto™, and a Bang & Olufsen 12-speaker sound system. Safety features include Lane-Keeping System, Blind Spot Information System, and Automatic Emergency Braking, with optional Adaptive Cruise Control and Parking Assist.
