Mazda CX-5

The 2025 Mazda CX-5 is a compact SUV with a 2.5L Inline-4 SKYACTIV-G engine producing 187 hp, and an optional 2.5L Turbocharged Inline-4 SKYACTIV-G engine with 256 hp. It features a 6-speed shiftable automatic transmission and all-wheel drive.

The CX-5 has a length of 180.1 inches, a width of 72.6 inches, and a height of 66.1 inches, with a wheelbase of 106.2 inches and a cargo capacity of 30.8 cubic feet (rear seats up) and 59.3 cubic feet (rear seats down). The interior includes a 9-inch touchscreen, Mazda Connect™, Apple CarPlay™, and Android Auto™, with advanced safety features like Blind-Spot Warning and Rear Cross-Traffic Alert.

2025 Mazda 3

The 2025 Mazda 3 is a compact car with a 2.5L Inline-4 SKYACTIV-G engine producing 191 hp, and an optional 2.5L Turbocharged Inline-4 SKYACTIV-G engine with up to 250 hp. It features a 6-speed shiftable automatic transmission and offers both front-wheel drive (FWD) and all-wheel drive (AWD).

The Mazda 3 has a length of 183.5 inches, a width of 70.7 inches, and a height of 56.9 inches, with a wheelbase of 107.3 inches and a cargo capacity of 13.2 cubic feet (rear seats up) or 36.5 cubic feet (rear seats down). The interior includes an 8.8-inch touchscreen display, Mazda Connect™, Apple CarPlay™, and Android Auto™, with advanced safety features such as Blind-Spot Warning and Rear Cross-Traffic Alert.
