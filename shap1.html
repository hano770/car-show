The 2025 Aston Martin Vanquish is a grand tourer that combines luxury and performance. It features a 5.2L twin-turbocharged V12 engine producing 835 hp and 1,000 Nm of torque, accelerating from 0-60 mph in 3.3 seconds and reaching a top speed of 214 mph. The Vanquish has a rear-wheel drive system, an 8-speed automatic transmission, and a wheelbase of 114 inches.

It offers a spacious interior with heated and ventilated front seats, a surround-view camera, and advanced safety features like blindspot detection and adaptive cruise control. The car's exterior boasts carbon fiber bodywork and a bespoke chassis for unmatched dynamics.

Bugatti Tourbillon

The Bugatti Tourbillon is an upcoming hyper sports car set to be released in 2026. It features a naturally aspirated 8.3L V16 engine developed by Cosworth, producing 1,000 hp at 9,000 rpm and 900 Nm of torque. The car also includes three electric motors (two at the front axle and one at the rear), providing an additional 800 hp and 985 Nm of torque, resulting in a combined output of 1,800 hp and 1,985 Nm of torque.

The Tourbillon can accelerate from 0-100 km/h in 2.0 seconds, 0-200 km/h in under 5 seconds, and 0-300 km/h in under 10 seconds, with a top speed of 445 km/h (276 mph).
