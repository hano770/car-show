BMW 5 SERIES SEDAN

The BMW 5 Series offers a choice of engines: a 2.0L 4-cylinder Turbo (530i) with 255 hp, a 3.0L 6-cylinder Turbo (540i) with 335 hp, and a 3.0L 6-cylinder Plug-in Hybrid (550e xDrive) with 450 hp. It features an 8-speed shiftable automatic transmission and comes in rear-wheel drive (530i) and all-wheel drive (xDrive models). Dimensions include a length of 199.2 inches, a width of 74.8 inches, and a height of 59.6 inches. The wheelbase is 117.9 inches, and it offers a cargo capacity of 18.4 cubic feet with a 15.9-gallon fuel tank.

Inside, the 5 Series accommodates five passengers and comes equipped with a BMW Curved Display, Harman Kardon Premium Sound System, Apple CarPlay, and Android Auto™. Safety features include Lane Departure Warning, Dynamic Stability Control, and Automatic Emergency Braking, with optional Highway Assistant and Parking Assistant.

BMW M5 Touring

The 2025 BMW M5 Touring features a powerful 4.4L V8 engine with M TwinPower Turbo technology and an electric motor, delivering a combined 717 hp and 1,000 Nm of torque. It accelerates from 0-60 mph in just 3.5 seconds and has a top speed of 155 mph (190 mph with the M Driver’s Package)

The M5 Touring offers all-wheel drive (M xDrive), a spacious interior with seating for five, and a cargo capacity of 500-1,630 liters. It also includes advanced features like adaptive suspension, M-specific interior lighting, and high-performance brakes.
