Rolls-Royce Phantom Series II

The Rolls-Royce Phantom Series II is a luxury sedan that epitomizes elegance and sophistication. It features a 6.7L V12 engine producing 453.5 bhp and 720 Nm of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Phantom Series II offers rear-wheel drive, a wheelbase of 123 inches, and a length of 196.4 inches.

It boasts a 22-inch wheel design, rear-hinged doors, and a spacious interior with rear-hinged suicide doors for easy access to the rear seats. The car also includes advanced driver assistance systems, climate control, and premium leather upholstery.
