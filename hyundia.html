IONIQ 9

The 2025 Hyundai IONIQ 9 is an all-electric SUV with a 110.3 kWh battery providing a range of 495 km in mild weather and 355 km in cold weather. It features a rear-wheel drive system, accelerates from 0-100 km/h in 9.4 seconds, and has a top speed of 185 km/h.

The IONIQ 9 offers a spacious interior with seating for seven passengers, a 13.8-inch infotainment touchscreen, and advanced safety features like Blind-Spot Warning and Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist.
